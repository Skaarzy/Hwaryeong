## **Partnership 1: The Iron-Royal Accord (철왕 협정 - Cheolwang Hyeopjeong)**

**Partners:**
- Iron Storm Pact (Heavenly Sword, Divine Thunder Hall)
- Royal Compact (Jeong Dynasty + 5 Clans)

**Type:** Political-Military Alliance
**Bond:** Mutual benefit through complementary strengths. Iron Storm provides martial prestige and warrior excellence, representing the peak of orthodox warrior culture. Royal Compact provides economic networks, trade routes, and logistical support through the specialized clan services.

**What They Offer Each Other:**
- **Iron Storm receives:** Trade supplies, economic stability, access to maritime routes (Baek Clan), trade route security (Yu Clan), and financial backing (Han Clan)
- **Royal Compact receives:** Martial legitimacy, warrior prestige, elite combat support, and respect from orthodox sects through association with legendary warriors

**Shared Interest:** Both rival Shadow Covenant. Both value strength and authority - Iron Storm through martial prowess, Royal Compact through political and economic power.
**Nature:** Public, formal alliance. Practical partnership built on mutual respect for different forms of power.

## **Partnership 2: The Wanderer's Trade Pact (유랑 무역 서약 - Yurang Muyeok Seoyak)**

**Partners:**
- Eastern Prosperity Accord (Plum Blossom, Li Trading, Heavenly Root)
- Unbound Brotherhood (Wind Cloud, Iron Palm)

**Type:** Economic-Protection Alliance
**Bond:** Pragmatic cooperation without political entanglement. Eastern Accord needs protection for trade routes in wilderness and border regions and the Unbound Brotherhood helps. Unbound Brotherhood needs supplies, medicine, and trade goods without political strings attached.

**What They Offer Each Other:**
- **Eastern Accord receives:** Trade route security, safe passage through wilderness territories, protection from bandits without involving government forces.
- **Unbound Brotherhood receives:** Medical supplies, trade goods, economic resources, no political demands or interference in their autonomy.

**Shared Interest:** Both avoid heavy political involvement. Eastern Accord focuses on commerce, Unbound Brotherhood rejects all politics. Perfect match of non-interference.
**Nature:** Informal, practical partnership. Respectful distance maintained - cooperation without integration.

## **Partnership 3: The Throne and Scholar Alliance (왕좌 학자 동맹 - Wangjwa Hakja Dongmaeng)**

**Partners:**
- Royal Compact (Jeong Dynasty + 5 Clans)
- Western Threefold Path (Divine Seal, Jade Orchid, Frost Moon)

**Type:** Political-Scholarly Alliance
**Bond:** Complementary authority structures. Royal Compact provides political legitimacy, economic power, and military force. Western Threefold provides scholarly prestige, spiritual authority, and mystical knowledge. Divine Seal Sect already serves as court advisors, making this partnership a natural extension of existing informal cooperation.

**What They Offer Each Other:**
- **Royal Compact receives:** Scholarly legitimacy, spiritual authority, mystical knowledge (talismans, demon sealing), cultural refinement, intellectual prestige
- **Western Threefold receives:** Political backing, royal protection, economic resources, official recognition, access to court influence

**Shared Interest:** Both rival Unbound Brotherhood and Shadow Covenant. Both value order, refinement, and proper civilization. Both want to shape society - Royal Compact through political/economic means, Western Threefold through education and spiritual cultivation.
**Nature:** Formal alliance with deep ties. Possibly the strongest partnership due to existing court connections and perfectly aligned rivals.
