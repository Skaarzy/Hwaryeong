# **POST-ETERNAL WAR ALLIANCES 

## **Alliance 1: The Four Pillars Alliance (사주 동맹 - Saju Dongmaeng)**

**Geographic Position:** Spread across eastern and western provinces **Members:**
- Plum Blossom Sect - 
- Heavenly Sword Sect
- Jade Orchid Sect
- Frost Moon Sect

**Type:** Traditional Orthodox Elite Alliance
**Philosophy:** Order through law and control prevents chaos. These four represent the surviving pillars of the original five great orthodox sects. After witnessing the devastation of the Eternal War, they believe the catastrophe occurred because there was **insufficient control and enforcement of martial law**. If the Divine Vow had been stricter, if surveillance had been tighter, if authority had been more centralized - the Heavenly Demon might never have risen. They advocate for **stronger Martial Council authority, stricter sect regulations, and centralized oversight** to prevent future disasters.

**Complementary Strengths:**
- **Plum Blossom:** Medicinal knowledge, elegant sword arts, cultural refinement, diplomatic grace
- **Heavenly Sword:** Elite swordsmanship, strategic wisdom, warrior honor code, martial leadership
- **Jade Orchid:** Female cultivation mastery, elegant martial arts, cultural influence, independent strength
- **Frost Moon:** Mental discipline, qi control, telekinetic techniques, philosophical teaching, scholarly depth
**Bond:** Mutual survival and shared conviction that order prevents catastrophe. These four sects survived the Eternal War together, witnessed the fall of Divine Seal Sect and the banishment of Heavenly Root Sect. They are united by trauma and the belief that **stricter laws and greater control** would have prevented the war's horrors. They see themselves as guardians not just of orthodox traditions, but of **martial order itself**.

**Alliance Purpose:**
- Advocate for **stronger Martial Council authority** and enforcement powers
- Push for **stricter sect regulations** and oversight mechanisms
- Establish **centralized monitoring** to detect threats early
- Preserve orthodox martial traditions through **institutional control**
- Ensure no sect can operate without accountability (prevent another Heavenly Demon situation)
**Martial Council Stance:** **Pro-law, pro-control faction.** They vote for increased regulations, mandatory sect inspections, stricter punishment for Divine Vow violations, and centralized authority. They believe freedom without oversight leads to chaos and tragedy.

## **Alliance 2: The Iron Brotherhood (철형제단 - Cheol Hyeongjedan)**

**Geographic Position:** Border provinces, wilderness territories, independent regions **Members:**
- Iron Palm Sect
- Divine Thunder Hall
- Wind Cloud Pavilion
- Li Trading Company

**Type:** Pragmatic Freedom Alliance
**Philosophy:** Freedom and autonomy prevent tyranny. These sects believe the Eternal War happened **because there was too much control and too little freedom**. The Jeong Dynasty's oppressive interference, orthodox sects' rigid enforcement, and centralized authority created the conditions for rebellion and resentment that birthed the Heavenly Demon. They argue that **excessive control breeds desperation and violence**. If sects and individuals had more freedom to live as they chose, if laws were less restrictive, if authority was less suffocating - the war might never have happened. They advocate for **minimal Martial Council interference, individual sect autonomy, and personal freedom**.

**Complementary Strengths:**
- **Iron Palm:** Defensive mastery, body conditioning, durability, internal power cultivation
- **Divine Thunder:** Coordinated assault, explosive power, military discipline, overwhelming force (less isolationist after the war)
- **Wind Cloud:** Speed, mobility, evasion, border defense experience, perfect timing
- **Li Trading Company:** Economic networks, merchant connections, supply lines, diplomatic relations, resource management
**Bond:** Shared conviction that freedom prevents oppression-driven catastrophe. During and after the Eternal War, these sects concluded that **rigid control and centralized authority** created the environment for disaster. Divine Thunder Hall, once extremely isolationist, opened up after witnessing how isolation and control led to suffering. They found kinship with sects who valued **individual autonomy, personal choice, and minimal interference** in how sects and disciples live their lives.

**Alliance Purpose:**
- Advocate for **minimal Martial Council interference** in sect affairs
- Protect **individual sect autonomy** and personal freedom
- Oppose **excessive regulations and centralized oversight**
- Support disciples who want to live ordinary lives without persecution
- Provide economic stability through Li Trading's networks (independence through self-sufficiency)
- Challenge the Four Pillars' push for control whenever it threatens freedom
**Martial Council Stance:** **Anti-control, pro-freedom faction.** They vote against increased regulations, oppose mandatory inspections, resist centralized authority expansion, and advocate for sect independence. They believe control without freedom leads to oppression and rebellion.
**Alliance Dynamic:** Unlike the Four Pillars' formal hierarchy, the Iron Brotherhood operates as equals. Each sect maintains full autonomy, cooperating when needed but never demanding obedience. They practice what they preach - **freedom within alliance**.

## **Alliance 3: The Shadow Covenant (그림자 서약 - Geurimja Seoyak)**

**Geographic Position:** Isolated territories far from orthodox sect influence **Members:**
- Shadow-Ink Assassins
- Withered Lotus Sect
- Iron Fist Sect (NEW member post-war)

**Type:** Unorthodox Survival Coalition
**Philosophy:** Survival through unity and unconventional power. After the Eternal War exposed how vulnerable isolated unorthodox sects were to orthodox persecution, the Shadow Covenant expanded. They embrace techniques orthodox sects condemn, operate in territories where orthodox influence is weak, and protect each other from combined orthodox assault. Not evil - just different, pragmatic, and refusing to conform to orthodox definitions of "proper" martial arts.

**Complementary Strengths:**
- **Shadow-Ink Assassins:** Psychological warfare, stealth, assassination, patience, fear tactics, intelligence networks
- **Withered Lotus:** Life-draining techniques, poison mastery, decay arts, entropy cultivation, body restoration
- **Iron Fist Sect:** Warrior culture, separation from society, martial strength, combat mastery, defensive power
**Bond:** Mutual protection and shared outsider status. The Eternal War proved that unorthodox sects cannot survive alone. When orthodox sects united against threats, unorthodox sects were easy targets. Iron Fist Sect, newly formed by warriors who rejected both orthodox rigidity and mainstream society, sought protection and found it in the Shadow Covenant. All three sects are feared, misunderstood, and targeted by orthodox persecution. Alliance ensures no unorthodox sect faces extinction alone.

**Alliance Purpose:**
- Prevent unorthodox sect extinction (like what happened to Heavenly Root)
- Share intelligence about orthodox sect movements and threats
- Provide refuge for disciples fleeing orthodox persecution
- Maintain "live and let live" understanding with each other
- Stand together if orthodox sects attempt coordinated assault

**Martial Council Stance:** **NOT in Martial Council** - unorthodox sects are excluded. However, they monitor Council decisions through Beggars Sect intelligence and Shadow-Ink networks. They are **de facto anti-control** because any increase in Martial Council authority threatens them directly.
**Alliance Dynamic:** Loosely organized but fiercely loyal when threatened. They don't meet regularly or coordinate daily operations. Each sect maintains complete autonomy in their isolated territories. BUT if one is attacked, the others respond. Intelligence sharing is constant through Shadow-Ink's networks. They operate under the principle: "We are hated separately, but feared together."
**Post-War Addition - Iron Fist Sect:** The newest member, Iron Fist, needed protection after forming post-war. As warriors who separated from mainstream martial society, they fit perfectly into the Shadow Covenant's outsider identity. Shadow-Ink and Withered Lotus saw a kindred spirit - another sect refusing to bow to orthodox expectations. Iron Fist brings raw martial power to balance Shadow-Ink's subtlety and Withered Lotus's forbidden techniques.

# **INDEPENDENT ENTITIES (Not in Alliances):**

## **Serene Mountain Sect**
- **Status:** Buddhist monks occupying former Heavenly Root territory
- **Relationship:** Land under Jeong Empire control, but monks are independent
- **Stance:** No alliances, no rivalries, neutral existence
- **Purpose:** Spiritual cultivation, guarding the imprisoned Heavenly Demon, maintaining peace
- **Note:** Not in Martial Council, don't participate in sect politics

## **Beggars Sect**
- **Status:** Independent information brokers
- **Relationship:** Neutral to all alliances
- **Stance:** In Martial Council but avoids taking political sides
- **Purpose:** Information gathering and selling, profit-focused
- **Note:** Abandoned imperial territory locations, operates only in sect provinces

## **Jeong Empire**
- **Status:** Political entity, no longer connected to martial alliances
- **Relationship:** Absorbed the 5 clans, expanded territory, separated from orthodox sects
- **Stance:** Banned martial arts in imperial provinces, governs through political authority not martial power
- **Note:** Not in Martial Council, the divide between empire and sects is complete
